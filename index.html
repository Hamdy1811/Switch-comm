The Core Objective

The existing OLA infrastructure is a powerful asset, yet it faces challenges in consumer perception, footfall, and brand loyalty. Our objective is to transform OLA from a transactional fuel stop into a lifestyle destination people visit, talk about, and return to, starting with 4-5 high-visibility stations.

OLA's Destination Strategy

"OLA is not just a place to refuel — it’s your everyday stop for service, convenience, and care. We make the moments between destinations easier, friendlier, and more rewarding."

OLA Café Corners

Express coffee booths with shaded seating, USB charging, and quick snacks.

Loyalty Zones

Digital QR codes at every station for customers to earn redeemable Fuel Points via the OLA App.

"OLA Care Reports"

Free on-ground car checks for tires, oil, and fluids by polite, professional staff.

Activating the Experience: Switch's Capabilities in Action

Here’s how we link OLA's desired strategies with Switch's specific "Store Activation" capabilities to bring the vision to life.

On-Ground Activation / Roadshow Energy

Concept: Reintroduce OLA in-person with high-energy, seasonal roadshows at high-traffic stations.

Value: Creates immediate buzz, personal connection, and direct engagement.

How Switch Helps: Décor, Lighting, Sound, Live DJ/MC, Event Management, Production, Kit Giveaways.

"Fastest Bite in Town!" Hotdog Booths

Concept: Branded mini-booths serving the "Fastest, Tastiest & Cheapest Hotdog in Town!" to act as a traffic generator.

Value: Drives curious footfall, generates viral content, and makes OLA a fun destination.

How Switch Helps: Specialized Décor, Branding, Sound, Live Entertainment Integration, Organizers.

"Walk-By Win" Event Gantries

Concept: Visual "event zone" structures with rotating messages and interactive elements like Spin & Win kiosks.

Value: Converts passive foot traffic into active participants and generates word-of-mouth.
